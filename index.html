Agni-5 missile

India has successfully conducted night trails of nuclear-capable ballistic missile Agni V. The test was actually carried out to validate new technologies and equipment.The flight performance of the missile were tracked and monitored by radars, range stations and tracking systems. Agni-5 has been successfully tested multiple times since 2012.

Key points:

  • The development of Agni missiles was started in early 1980.
  • The missile was fired from Abdul Kalam Island , off the coast of Odisha.
